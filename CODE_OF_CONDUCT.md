# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users of OSSA, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

We expect all participants to:

- **Be respectful** — Treat others with kindness and professionalism
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome diverse perspectives and ideas
- **Be constructive** — Provide helpful feedback and criticism
- **Be honest** — Communicate openly and transparently
- **Be collaborative** — Work together to solve problems

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or dehumanizing language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Unwelcome sexual or romantic advances
- Trolling, spam, or off-topic disruption
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all OSSA spaces:
- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Code repositories and commit messages
- Documentation and comments
- Any official OSSA events or channels

## Reporting & Enforcement

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document it** — Note what happened, when, and who was involved
2. **Report it** — Contact the OSSA maintainers at the repository
3. **We will respond** — Within 48 hours with acknowledgment

All reports will be:
- **Taken seriously**
- **Handled confidentially**
- **Investigated promptly**
- **Resolved fairly**

Violations may result in:
- A private warning
- Comment removal or edit
- Temporary or permanent ban from participation

Have questions about this Code of Conduct? Open an issue or reach out to the OSSA maintainers.
